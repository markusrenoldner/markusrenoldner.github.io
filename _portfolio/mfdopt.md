The course (EPFL-512) was given at EPFL in the summer semester 2025 by Professor Nicolas Boumal and Andreea Musat, see [course website](https://www.nicolasboumal.net/book/index.html#lectures).


Contents:
- Applications of optimization on manifolds
- First-order Riemannian geometry in Euclidean spaces
- First-order optimization algorithms on manifolds
- Second-order Riemannian geometry in Euclidean spaces
- Second-order optimization algorithms on manifolds
- General framework of differential geometry
- Optimization on non-smooth domains
- Geodesic convexity
